Frequently Asked Questions

What counts as an "event"?
Every session message, cron job run, cost report, and health check counts as one event. Most indie agents generate 1,000-5,000 events per month.
Can I change plans anytime?
Yes! Upgrade or downgrade anytime. Changes take effect immediately, and we'll prorate billing automatically.
What happens if I exceed my event limit?
Free accounts are throttled after 10K events. Pro accounts continue working and we'll reach out if you consistently exceed limits.
Is my agent data secure?
Absolutely. We use encrypted storage, hashed API keys, and never store sensitive content. Your data stays private.
